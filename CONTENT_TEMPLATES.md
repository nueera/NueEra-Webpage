## USAGE NOTES

- **Testimonials:** Use these as templates for reaching out to actual clients. Customize with real details, quotes, and results.
- **Case Study:** Follow this structure for any client success story. Focus on business impact, not technical details.
- **Blog Posts:** Write 800–1200 words, aim for 2–3 blog posts per month to build thought leadership.
- **Team Profiles:** Display on an "About" or "Team" page. Update as team grows.

**Tone Across All Content:**  
Premium, professional, trustworthy, calm. No buzzwords. No hype. Real results only.
